XLingPaper Guide

by NuclearChickens

XLingPaper is a powerful tool for writing and editing linguistic papers developed by Andy Black at SIL.

XLingPaper automatically numbers sections and examples and keeps track of section references, citation references, and glosses/abbreviations.
